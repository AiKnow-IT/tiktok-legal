Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

AIKnow-IT ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data when you use the AIKnow-IT platform and services (the "Service"). This policy applies to all users of our website and platform.

AIKnow-IT is operated from the Netherlands and complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Regulation (EU) 2016/679.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

AIKnow-IT
Email: business.ai.gpt@gmail.com
Location: The Netherlands

3. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

3.1 Account Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and communication)
  • Name (if provided)

3.2 Social Media Platform Data

When you connect your social media accounts, we collect and store:

  • TikTok: OAuth access token, refresh token, TikTok user ID (open_id). We only request the video.publish scope. We do not access your private videos, messages, followers list, or personal profile data beyond what is required for publishing.
  • YouTube: OAuth access token, refresh token, YouTube channel ID. Used exclusively for uploading videos to your channel.
  • Instagram: Access token, Instagram Business Account ID. Used exclusively for publishing Reels to your account.

3.3 Generated Content Data

  • AI-generated video files
  • AI-generated scripts, captions, and hashtags
  • Publishing status and metadata (timestamps, platform post IDs)

3.4 Usage Data

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Feature usage patterns (videos generated, platforms published to)

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To generate videos and publish them to your connected social media accounts on your behalf.
  • Authentication: To securely connect and maintain access to your social media accounts through OAuth.
  • Communication: To send you important notifications about your account, publishing status, and service updates.
  • Service improvement: To understand how users interact with our platform and improve the user experience.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell your data to third parties, or use your social media data for any purpose other than the specific publishing functionality you have authorized.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): When you connect your social media accounts through OAuth, you explicitly consent to us publishing content on your behalf.
  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing necessary to provide the Service you have signed up for.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): For service improvement and security monitoring, balanced against your privacy rights.

6. Data Sharing

We share your data with the following categories of third parties, solely to provide the Service:

  • TikTok (ByteDance): Video content and metadata are sent via the Content Posting API for publishing to your TikTok account.
  • YouTube (Google): Video content and metadata are sent via the YouTube Data API for uploading to your channel.
  • Instagram (Meta): Video content and metadata are sent via the Instagram Graph API for publishing Reels.
  • OpenAI: Text prompts (containing no personal data) are sent for AI content generation. No user personal data is shared with OpenAI.
  • Supabase (Hosting): Video files are temporarily stored for the purpose of providing public URLs required by social media publishing APIs.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

7. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens: Stored as long as your account is active and the social media connection is maintained. Immediately deleted upon disconnection or account deletion.
  • Generated videos: Stored for up to 90 days after publishing, then automatically deleted from our servers.
  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 12 months for security and service improvement, then anonymized or deleted.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing (Art. 18): Request limitation of processing.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time by disconnecting your social media accounts or deleting your account.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at business.ai.gpt@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. TikTok-Specific Disclosures

In compliance with TikTok's developer requirements:

  • We only access TikTok data that you have explicitly authorized through the OAuth consent flow.
  • We only use the video.publish scope to post content on your behalf.
  • We do not store or share your TikTok data with any party other than TikTok itself (for publishing).
  • You can revoke our access at any time through TikTok's app settings or by disconnecting in AIKnow-IT.
  • All content posted through our platform complies with TikTok's Community Guidelines.

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
  • Secure storage of OAuth tokens using encrypted credential management
  • Access controls limiting who can access user data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

For more details, see our Security page.

11. Children's Privacy

AIKnow-IT is a platform for content creators — not a platform for children. Our Service is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. The content generated by our platform is designed to be safe for children to watch, but the platform itself is operated by adult creators and publishers.

12. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through a notice on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.

14. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Email: business.ai.gpt@gmail.com
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 business days.
