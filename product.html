Product

AIKnow-IT helps teams manage short-form content creation and publishing.

Key features

  • Automated short-video generation with kid-safe guidelines.
  • Caption/hashtag preparation per platform.
  • Publishing workflow for TikTok (and other platforms).
  • Status tracking and error notifications.

User control

  • Users connect their own TikTok account via OAuth.
  • Users can change privacy settings and revoke access anytime.
