Safety policy (kids content)

AIKnow-IT is designed for kid-friendly short videos intended for ages 8–12. Our goal is positive, educational, and entertaining content.

Allowed themes

  • Friendship, teamwork, kindness
  • Curiosity, learning, problem solving
  • Light humor and adventure
  • Positive moral lessons (age-appropriate)

Prohibited content

  • Adult or sexual content
  • Graphic violence, gore, or harmful instructions
  • Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or discrimination
  • Illegal activities, drugs, or self-harm content
  • Real-person impersonation or use of public figures
  • Copyrighted characters/music when not licensed

How we enforce safety

  • Prompt guidelines: generation prompts include strict kid-safe rules.
  • Metadata controls: titles/captions are generated with age-appropriate language.
  • Workflow checks: content is validated before publishing (e.g., banned themes/keywords).
  • Human oversight option: creators can pause publishing if needed.

Users remain responsible for final content and for compliance with platform policies.

Report a safety issue
